Paper: Sports Impact on Educational Institutions

As an athlete my whole life, until sophmore year at Trine, I have always wondered how impactful sports are on our educational systems. I decided to look at universities and colleges. I was sure to look at smaller universities, like Trine, at the Division 3 level, and then with larger schools at the Division 1 level to see the differences in how sports plays a role on campus. In the paper, I find that there are in fact major differences in how the sport is played, and how the athletes are treated or used on campus.Can also download the paper here .
